# MyFeedAI

A privacy-focused RSS reader featuring local storage, custom AI integration for summaries and translation, and flexible data import options.

## Logo
- src/lib/assets/logo.png


## Features
- Local-First & Privacy-Focused: All data is stored locally on your device to ensure your privacy and security. No data tracking.
- Custom AI Providers: Supports configuration of custom AI providers (BYO-AI), giving you full control over the model you use.
- AI-Powered Insights: Built-in AI capabilities for instant article summarization and seamless translation.
- Versatile Import Options: Supports multiple methods for importing feed sources, including OPML and more.
